# AI-Powered Insurance for India’s Gig Economy

## Introduction
The gig worker economy is rapidly expanding, and our reliance on it continues to grow. Yet, the livelihoods of these workers remain largely unprotected, with income streams that are highly vulnerable to external disruptions such as extreme weather, platform outages, and demand fluctuations.

Our chosen persona is food delivery partners. We focus on this group because their income is highly sensitive to multiple uncontrollable factors on a daily basis. Despite these challenges, they continue to operate without any form of protection against short-term income loss. This makes them an ideal candidate for a parametric, data-driven insurance solution that can provide timely and fair compensation.

## External Factors
### Environmental Factors:
  **Extreme Heat:** Extreme heat limits working hours 
  
  **Heavy Rainfall:** Heavy rain reduces order volume and slows deliveries     
  
  **High AQI:** Poor Air Quality creates visibility issues and causes delays     
  
  Due to climate change, wetaher disruptions are only expected to get worse in the future.

### Traffic: 
Common problem in most Indian cities which leads to delay in orders and unproductive waiting time

### Platform Reliability Factors
  **High Server Latency**: Slow response times delay or prevent order assignment

  **App Crashes / Downtime**: Workers are unable to receive or accept orders

  **Order Assignment Failures**: Backend issues prevent orders from being allocated

### Civic and Regulatory
  **Curfews / Strikes / Movement restrictions**: unable to access pickup and drop locations and possibility of order disruptions

## Financial Model
The financial model is structured on a weekly payout basis to match the typical gig worker's payout schedule.
